Precision medicine depends on rapidly retrieving the most relevant literature for a patient's specific molecular profile. This project develops **neural retrieval and representation learning** methods tailored to the biomedical domain.

Our work spans improved biomedical word embeddings, joint learning for named entity recognition and normalization, and document ranking systems that combine neural matching with faceted summarization signals. We have participated in the TREC Precision Medicine track and evaluated our systems against community benchmarks.

### Key Contributions

- Improved biomedical word embeddings evaluated across multiple downstream tasks
- Joint NER and entity normalization framework for clinical text
- Literature retrieval system for precision medicine using neural matching and faceted summarization (EMNLP 2020)
